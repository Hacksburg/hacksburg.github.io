Contact Us

Email

People needing to speak to someone "in charge" (donation offers, media requests, outside event proposals, etc) should email the Board of Directors at board at hacksburg dot org. Messages are checked often, and a Board member will get back to you as soon as they can.

Mailing List

We have a mailing list through Mailchimp where we post blog posts and announcements. This is one of the best ways to keep abreast of what's happening at the space.

Facebook

We have a very active Facebook group where a lot of our community hangs out and discusses various topics related to making things. If you have a question for the community, posting it here is your best bet to get a response. Membership in the Hacksburg Facebook group is open to anyone, though you need to be approved by a group administrator first, to cut down on spam.

We also have a separate Facebook page for Hacksburg, where we post announcements. We do receive messages from here, but response time is slower than email; if your need is urgent, email is your better bet.

Twitter

We have a Twitter feed where we post announcements and converse with other tweeters.
