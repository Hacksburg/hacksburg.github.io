Hacksburg is Blacksburg's Makerspace
Hacksburg is a community workshop located in Blacksburg, Virginia. Through instructional classes and weekly work sessions, we aim to help members of the Blacksburg community complete projects that would otherwise be out of reach.

Hacksburg's woodshop, metalshop, laser cutter, 3D printers, electronics bench, and CNC mill are available for use by Hacksburg members twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week.

Hacksburg's weekly meeting is open to the public every Tuesday at 7:30pm (online and in person).

Resin Printing Basics
Learn to use Hacksburg's 8K SLA resin printer

Filament-based 3D printers are capable of making an incredible array of parts, but, for high-detail miniatures, our new resin printer is capable of over 10x the resolution. In this class, you'll learn how to use Hacksburg's resin printer, which kind of parts are best-suited to resin printing, and how these machines differ from filament-based printers.

This class is for ages 16 and up. Attendees under 18 must be accompanied by a parent/guardian for the full duration of the class, and parent/guardian must approve all equipment use.
