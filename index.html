Hacksburg is Blacksburg's Makerspace
Hacksburg is a community workshop located in Blacksburg, Virginia. Through instructional classes and weekly work sessions, we aim to help members of the Blacksburg community complete projects that would otherwise be out of reach.

Hacksburg's woodshop, metalshop, laser cutter, 3D printers, electronics bench, and CNC mill are available for use by Hacksburg members twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week.

Hacksburg's weekly meeting is open to the public every Tuesday at 7:30pm (online and in person).
