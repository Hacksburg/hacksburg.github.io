Made in Blacksburg
From rockets to robots to rocking chairs, whatever it is, you can probably make it at Hacksburg. Our community workshop is located in Virginia Tech's Corportate Research Center at 1872 Pratt Drive, and accessible by Hacksburg members twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week.

Our weekly meeting is open to the public every Tuesday at 7:30pm (online and in person). Stop by to learn about the space, make friends, or share what you're working on.

To learn more about the tools Hacksburg offers, check out our wiki or take a 360° Virtual Tour.
