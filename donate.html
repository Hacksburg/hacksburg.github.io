Support Hacksburg

Hacksburg is Blacksburg’s first makerspace: a community workshop offering tools, training, and mentorship enabling the NRV community to innovate, educate, and create. Hacksburg also offers a variety of STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics) classes and events to community members of all ages, such as building basic electric circuits, 3D printing, cider making, and basic woodworking to name just a few.

Hacksburg is a 501c3 non-profit organization, fully funded through membership fees, donations, and grants. Through your support Hacksburg can provide greater opportunities to the Blacksburg community while helping to educate the next generation of inventors and creators. You can help support Hacksburg's mission without spending a cent through Amazon Smile, Kroger Community Rewards, or through the donation of tools and equipment.

Equipment Donation

We are always looking for new equipment (new and used!) for our workshop. A few examples; vinyl cutter, metal break, micrometers, etc. Reach out to board@hacksburg.org if you feel you might have equipment to donate or lend! If you'd like to purchase a tool for Hacksburg please see our Wish List.

Monetary Donations

Monetary donations give Hacksburg the most flexibility to advance our programs and offerings

  • PayPal
    • We are part of the PayPal Giving Fund - you can select us as a charity on PayPal partners like eBay and Humble Bundle.
  • Cash (stop by our open meetings, held every Tuesday, or attend an event!)
  • Check (made out to "Hacksburg" and mailed to Hacksburg, 1872 Kraft Drive, Suite 1620, Blacksburg, VA)
  • #GivingTuesday on Facebook. All donations made on Giving Tuesday will be matched up to 100%!

Kroger Community Rewards

Do you shop at Kroger? You can help Hacksburg out every time you shop as we are enrolled with the Kroger Community Rewards Program.

To enroll your Kroger Plus Card:

  1. Visit Kroger Community Rewards (be sure to have your Kroger Plus Card handy!)
  2. Create a digital Kroger account. If you already have a digital account, simply link your Kroger Card to your account.
  3. Search for Hacksburg here by our name "Hacksburg" or our NPO number "DA497", then select organization from list and click "Save". You may need to change your prefered store to a local location to see Hacksburg listed.
  4. To verify you are enrolled correctly, you will see your organization’s name on the Kroger Community Rewards section of your account.
  5. All participants of an organization are required to re-enroll each year beginning August 1st.
  6. All purchases made when you swipe your Korger Plus Card or enter your account number will be applied to the program at no added cost to you!
